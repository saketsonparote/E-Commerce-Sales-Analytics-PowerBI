# 📊 Project Overview: E-Commerce Sales Analytics

## 📌 1. Introduction
The **E-Commerce Sales Analytics** project is an interactive Business Intelligence solution developed using **Power BI**. The dashboard provides comprehensive insights into sales performance, customer behavior, and product trends, enabling data-driven decision-making for business stakeholders.

This project demonstrates expertise in **data modeling**, **data transformation**, and **advanced analytics** using **DAX**.

---

## 🎯 2. Business Problem
E-commerce businesses generate vast amounts of transactional data. However, without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to:
- Monitor overall sales performance.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Identify top-performing products and regions.
- Track growth trends over time.
- Make informed strategic decisions.

This project addresses these challenges by transforming raw data into meaningful and interactive visual insights.

---

## 🎯 3. Project Objectives
- Analyze **year-wise and monthly sales trends**.
- Evaluate **customer engagement and retention**.
- Identify **top-performing products and locations**.
- Measure **key performance indicators (KPIs)** such as revenue, quantity sold, and average unit price.
- Enable **Year-over-Year (YoY)** performance comparison.
- Provide an **interactive and user-friendly dashboard** for stakeholders.

---

## 📊 4. Dashboard Pages

### 🔹 4.1 Year Wise Sales Analysis
This page provides a comprehensive overview of sales performance.

**Key Features:**
- Total Revenue with YoY growth.
- Total Quantity Sold with YoY comparison.
- Average Unit Price (ASP).
- Monthly Sales Trend.
- Geographical Sales Distribution.
- Top 5 Products by Quantity Sold.
- Top Spending Customers.
- Interactive Year Slicer.

### 🔹 4.2 Customers and Product Insights
This page focuses on customer behavior and product performance.

**Key Features:**
- Total and Active Customers.
- Returning Customers Percentage.
- Active Customers by Order Bucket.
- Product-wise Performance.
- Year-wise Quantity Sold.
- Month-wise Active Customers and Quantity Sold.
- Sales by Location.

---

## 🧩 5. Data Model

### ⭐ Star Schema Architecture
The project follows a **Star Schema** data model, which enhances query performance and simplifies analysis.

### 📌 Fact Table
- **fact_table.csv**: Contains transactional sales data such as quantity sold and total revenue.

### 📌 Dimension Tables
- **customer_dim.csv**: Customer demographic and segmentation details.
- **item_dim.csv**: Product-related information.
- **store_dim.csv**: Store and geographical details.
- **time_dim.csv**: Date attributes enabling time intelligence.
- **Trans_dim.csv**: Additional transaction-related information.

### 🔗 Relationships
All dimension tables are connected to the **fact table** through primary and foreign keys, enabling efficient filtering and aggregation.

---

## 📈 6. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

| KPI | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| **Total Sales** | Total revenue generated from all transactions |
| **Total Quantity** | Total number of units sold |
| **Average Unit Price** | Average selling price per unit |
| **Total Customers** | Number of unique customers |
| **Active Customers** | Customers who made at least one purchase |
| **Returning Customers %** | Percentage of customers with repeat purchases |
| **YoY Sales Growth** | Year-over-Year percentage change in sales |
| **YoY Quantity Growth** | Year-over-Year percentage change in quantity |

---

## 🛠️ 7. Tools and Technologies

| Tool | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| **Power BI Desktop** | Data visualization and dashboard development |
| **Power Query** | Data cleaning and transformation |
| **DAX** | Creation of calculated measures and KPIs |
| **CSV Files** | Data storage and input |
| **GitHub** | Version control and portfolio presentation |

---

## 🌟 8. Key Insights

- 📈 **Positive Year-over-Year Growth:** The business shows consistent growth in both revenue and quantity sold.
- 🏆 **Top-Performing Products:** A small group of products contributes significantly to overall sales.
- 🌍 **Regional Sales Distribution:** Certain cities or regions generate higher revenue, indicating potential market opportunities.
- 👥 **Customer Behavior:** A strong base of active customers with insights into repeat purchasing patterns.
- 📅 **Seasonal Trends:** Monthly analysis reveals fluctuations that can guide inventory and marketing strategies.
